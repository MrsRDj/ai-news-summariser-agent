KEY POINTS:
- Antidoom addresses doom loops that occur in reasoning models, which can lead to ineffective outputs.
- The FTPO method retrains only the problematic token initiating the loop, enhancing efficiency.
- On LFM2.5-2.6B models, doom-loop occurrences decreased from 10.2% to 1.4%; on Qwen3.5-4B, from 22.9% to 1%.
